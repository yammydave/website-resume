WEB DESIGN

My forte is not in the field of web design, however have appreciation for both form and function. Further, I am not the client; my own preferences matter not, yours do. It is my job to meet your website needs and wants. There is no shortage of artistically designed websites on the net from where I can gain inspiration to please my customers. Why reinvent the wheel?

Search Engine Optimisation

As a junior developer, I am eager to grow my reputation to further my career, I depend heavily on recommendations and gaining kudos. Therefore it is in both our interests that the site I build you, pulls in the traffic needed to help you keep your business ahead. By doing so, it will then indirectly build mine. What use is the most beautiful of sites if nobody sees it?

Clean Code

Optimised code, requires less room and runs faster, will would-be customers wait patiently for your site to load? W3C is the main standards organisation for the World Wide Web. The site I build you will be validated as meeting W3C standards and as such will proudly display their validation logos demonstrating professionalism to your clients.

Freedom

If code is difficult to read you could be tied into a business relationship with the site builder regardless of how that relationship may proceed in the future. My code is written such that anyone, suitably qualified and skilled, you appoint can maintain and manage your site, so you will always be in control. Is freedom to move should you desire important to you?
